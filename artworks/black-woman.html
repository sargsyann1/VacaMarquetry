The Work, in Full
Concept

Luminosity & Dignity

A portrait that confronts and reclaims the gaze. The subject does not pose — she simply is. Pale ash veneers carry a quality of light that no pigment can produce: a warmth native to the material itself, uncaught and unmanipulated. The face is not beautified. It is witnessed.

Process

620 Pieces of Light

Construction began with the lightest veneers — ash, maple, and holly — laid in the highlight regions. Walnut and wenge were cut last, pressed into shadow zones to give the face its three-dimensional depth. No two grain directions repeat within the facial plane. Each cut was made freehand, without laser or template, over approximately four weeks.

Reflection

 

“This work began as a commission and became something I needed to finish for its own sake. I did not know that until it was done.”

Certificate of Authenticity

Every original leaves the atelier with a hand-signed Certificate of Authenticity documenting the title, dimensions, piece count, year, and artist signature — your guarantee that this work is an unrepeatable, one-of-one original.

Shipping & Delivery

Each original is crated and dispatched worldwide via white-glove fine art courier, fully insured in transit. Typical delivery is 5–10 business days from dispatch. Installation guidance included.
