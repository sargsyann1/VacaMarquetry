Your Portrait, Step by Step

From Inquiry to Heirloom

01

Share Your Photograph

Send the photo you’d like recreated — a loved one, a pet, a moment that matters.

02

Receive Your Free Preview (Within 24 Hours)

We translate your photograph into a polygonal preview showing exactly how your portrait will be composed in wood — at no cost and with no commitment.

03

Approve or Refine

Review and request adjustments. One round of revision is included at no cost. We won’t cut a single piece of wood until you’re genuinely satisfied.

04

Secure Your Commission

A 50% deposit confirms your place in our production calendar. The remaining balance is due before shipping.

05

Your Portrait Is Hand-Crafted

Each veneer piece is individually selected and cut by hand — typically 3–6 weeks of concentrated work per commission.

06

Watch It Come to Life

Photo and video updates at key milestones throughout production — you see the work evolve in real time.

07

Final Approval

High-resolution photographs of your completed portrait, sent for your approval before we pack and ship.

08

White-Glove Delivery, Worldwide

Carefully crated and shipped with full tracking and insurance. We handle every detail.

09

Certificate of Authenticity

Your portrait arrives with a numbered, signed Certificate — permanently registered in our artist’s registry.
