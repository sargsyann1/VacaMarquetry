Private Collection Not for Sale

The Sovereign

Portrait · 2024 · Over 910 pieces · 60 × 90 cm

The largest and most complex work in the collection. Over 910 hand-cut veneer pieces placed over six weeks — a portrait of uncommon stillness and authority. Held by the artist as a permanent record of the work’s highest point.
Private Collection Not for Sale

Christ

Portrait · 255 hand-cut wood pieces

255 veneer pieces, placed with the precision of a devotional act. Completed as a personal work and gifted to the artist’s mother — a mark of faith, craft, and the intimacy of the earliest commissions. Not for sale. Not for replication.

Private Collection Not for Sale

Arctic Eagle

Animal · 412 hand-cut wood pieces

412 individually selected veneer pieces form a portrait of silent force — an eagle rendered in the cold grain of natural wood. Held in the private collection as a study in restraint and the expressive potential of natural material alone.
