The Artist

Sasun Sargsyan

My name is Sasun, and I am a marquetry artist. For me, wood is not just a material — it's a living canvas waiting to tell a story.

Portrait of the artist in the studio

Origins

My path into marquetry began with a fascination for the texture and history held inside a single sheet of wood veneer — the way grain, tone, and color shift from one tree to the next, no two ever quite alike.

Discovering Marquetry

Through the intricate craft of marquetry, I transform individual pieces of wood veneer into detailed works of art. My studio is where I bring visions to life, meticulously cutting and fitting each tiny piece to create portraits, landscapes, and geometric designs.

Philosophy

The process is a meditation — a slow, careful dance between precision and creativity. Every piece is a testament to the warmth, texture, and natural beauty of the wood itself.

What Collectors Should Know

Each work invites you to look closer — to appreciate the unique grain of every component, and to feel the history and dedication poured into every seam. Nothing leaves the studio that I would not be proud to sign.
